Oreo's Fave Movies

My top 3 movies of all time

AirBud

I love me a fun sports movie and this one lives up to the hype!
It's so inspiring to see a fellow dog utilize his strengths to help his bro out on his team. Two paws way up!

12 Pups of Christmas

I'm a huge fan of holiday movies like my mom and "bad" movies like my dad. This film made me laugh and cry and reminds me of how much I love my family.

Wicked

Now ordinarily I would have picked another dog movie as one of my favorites, but when I saw Wicked it mesmerized me. The key element that put this in my top 3 was the animal rights storyline - that really hit me to my core! And who doesn't like a good catchy musical?!
